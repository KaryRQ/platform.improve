Platform Improvement

Role: UX Research
Methods: Interviews, surveys, user personas, empathy map, user needs, journey map, user flows, brainstorming, wireframes, user testing

Objective

The goal of this project was to improve the experience of using the platform through which different company profiles interact with both employees and clients. This initiative aimed to enhance communication between areas and provide a better service to our customers.

Interviews and Survey

The first step was to approach the platform’s users to understand their pain points and how they performed their daily tasks. By interviewing them and observing firsthand how they used the tool, we identified several areas for improvement in both design and usability. Additionally, we launched a survey for employees who could not attend the interviews, which gave us valuable insights not captured during the sessions. With this information, we proceeded to the next phase of the process.

User Personas

Based on the data gathered, we created user personas. Two main profiles were identified as the first groups to interact with the web platform.

Empathy map

By getting to know our personas more deeply and understanding their contexts, we built empathy maps to capture their feelings, motivations, and frustrations, helping us define how to support them and improve their experience.

User needs

From these insights, we outlined the key needs of each profile, ensuring that the new platform design addressed their goals and pain points.

Journey Map

With the information consolidated, we created journey maps for each profile. These maps visualized how users interacted with the platform, highlighted their emotions at different steps, and revealed opportunities for improvement.

User flow

Once the research and journey maps were validated, we designed new user flows for the platform. Each workflow was mapped for the different profiles, shared with the corresponding areas, and iterated upon based on their feedback to improve the final experience.

Brainstorming

After completing the research phase, we conducted brainstorming sessions with the UI team to share findings and begin shaping wireframes.

Wireframes

Once the user flows were approved, we created wireframes to visualize how the information should be structured and presented, making the experience clearer and more intuitive for users.

Mockups

As the UI team developed the final mockups, we carried out usability and content reviews to refine the experience and ensure clarity before presenting the final solution to management and stakeholders.

User testing

Finally, we conducted another round of user testing with the updated platform. The results were positive: users expressed acceptance of the changes and appreciated the improvements. A brief training session was also provided to ensure users could fully leverage the new functionalities.
